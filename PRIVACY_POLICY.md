# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: November 28, 2025**

## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the Domain Extraction Coda Pack ("the Pack") handles data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

The Pack does **NOT**:
- Collect any personal information
- Store your email addresses or data
- Track your usage beyond Coda's standard analytics
- Transmit your data outside of Coda's infrastructure
- Share your data with third parties
- Use cookies or tracking technologies
- Maintain any databases or servers

## How the Pack Works

When you use the Pack:

1. **You provide input**: Email addresses or hostnames in your Coda doc
2. **Processing occurs locally**: The Pack processes your input within Coda's secure environment
3. **Results are returned**: Extracted domain information is displayed in your Coda doc
4. **No storage**: The Pack does not store, log, or retain any of your data

## Data You Control

All data processed by the Pack:
- Remains in your Coda document
- Is subject to Coda's privacy and security policies
- Is under your control at all times
- Can be deleted by removing the Pack or deleting your doc

## Third-Party Services

The Pack operates on the **Coda Platform**:
- Your data is processed within Coda's infrastructure
- Coda's Privacy Policy applies: [https://coda.io/trust/privacy](https://coda.io/trust/privacy)
- Coda's security practices apply: [https://coda.io/trust/security](https://coda.io/trust/security)

The Pack uses the **Public Suffix List**:
- The PSL is public data maintained by Mozilla
- No data is sent to Mozilla or PSL maintainers
- The PSL data is embedded in the Pack

## Open Source Transparency

The Pack is open source:
- Source code: [https://github.com/yura-panasenko/domain-extract-pack](https://github.com/yura-panasenko/domain-extract-pack)
- You can inspect exactly what the Pack does
- No hidden functionality or tracking

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Use the Pack without providing any personal information
- Stop using the Pack at any time
- Request information about the Pack's functionality
- Inspect the open source code

## Data Security

Security is provided by:
- **Coda's infrastructure**: Enterprise-grade security and encryption
- **No data transmission**: Your data stays within Coda
- **No third-party access**: We cannot access your data

## Children's Privacy

The Pack does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The Pack is designed for business use and processes only the data you explicitly provide.

## International Users

The Pack operates within Coda's infrastructure. For information about international data transfers and storage, please refer to [Coda's Privacy Policy](https://coda.io/trust/privacy).

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy by:
- Updating the "Effective Date"
- Posting the new policy in the GitHub repository
- Updating the Pack description in Coda (if feasible)

Material changes will be communicated through the GitHub repository.
